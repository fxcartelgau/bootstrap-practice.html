About us

The Man Behind The Movement
OUR STORY A FILIPINO LUXURY STREETWEAR BRAND

In an environment where rows and rows of sewing machines were lined up, Jacob Cruz sparked his aspiration to make a statement in the fashion industry. He took this and honed his ingenuity to make a brand that aspires individuals to have the life they want.

Richboyz is a luxury streetwear fashion brand that has created an iconic logo representing its unique style and quality. With a sleek and minimalist design, the Richboyz logo combines the brand name in a modern font, conveying a sense of sophistication and exclusivity. The logo has become a symbol of high-end streetwear and is highly sought after by fashion enthusiasts.

In conclusion, Richboyz is a brand that is committed to ethical and sustainable fashion. They understand the importance of creating clothing that is not only fashionable but also environmentally responsible. By choosing to support brands like Richboyz, we can make a positive impact on the fashion industry and the world around us.
